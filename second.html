JÖNKÖPING CITY

Jönköping is one of the biggest cities in the south of Sweden and it's known for its University... but not only!

What activities can you do?

In the summer time you can take a swim in the beautiful lake Vättern of the city, which is the second biggest lake of the country.
During the winter then, cold-related activities are the best ones. You can go ice-skating in one of the multiples locations all around the city, or you can challenge yourself by taking a class of hockey⛸

Where can you hang out?

The citycenter is a lovely space full of shops where you can go shopping for a whole day or if you prefer more nature-oriented activities, Jönköping can offer a lot of forests and park area to go hiking or simply playing with your kids

Where can you eat?

There are a thon of restaurants around the city: Swedish, Italian, Indian, Japanese, Thai etc...
